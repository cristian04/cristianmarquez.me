About me

I began to love IT when I was 14 years old. I was building batch programs on MSDOS and QBasic

I ran my own "startup" fixing computers and building cybers cafes years later.

Soon after I discovered Linux and I started playing with it. My first distro was Connectiva, and later on I discovered Debian, Slackware and Ututo, a distro from my City. Mount was manual by then and you needed to compile the kernel from time to time. I learn lots!

I started building and maintaining servers. I played with OpenMosix, a cluster management system, and with my first Virtual Machines. (VirtualPC and QEMU)

I built mobile applications, web pages, SOAP and REST API's. I learn how to test them and how to perform automated testing.

I also did penetration and security testing, infrastructure automation, Continuous Integration and Continous Deployment pipelines.

I started using Public Clouds providers, mainly AWS and Google Cloud.

I now focus on Cloud Computing, Cloud Native, DevOps and Site Reliability Engineering.
